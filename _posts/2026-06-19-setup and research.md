My first week at Lucidity focused heavily on familiarizing myself with core digital marketing terms and meeting with the founder to align on the technical goals for my role. My primary project was researching the specific requirements needed to register for API access across high-priority platforms, including TikTok, LinkedIn, and Shopify. I built out detailed documentation and actionable checklists in Obsidian so we can track the application processes with as little friction as possible. Getting these integrations secured is a major business objective, as lacking access to popular platforms can be a dealbreaker for prospective agencies. Expanding our API pipeline will directly increase the number of companies Lucidity can partner with. Finally, I identified the Google Analytics exam as the certification I will be pursuing for the duration of the internship to help further my understanding of these data ecosystems.
